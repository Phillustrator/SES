About us

We are PhD scientists with a cardiovascular research background in science, medicine, biomedical engineering or physiology. We have published in leading cardiovascular journals and have peer-reviewed extensively. We know the territory and the challenges that you face and can offer specialist assistance and skills to enhance your chances of success.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide a personal, friendly and attentive service that provides flexible, specialist support for you, your laboratory, and your career. We aim to build a strong relationship with our clients and to help them drive forward research in cardiovascular science and medicine.

We are specialists

We specialise in cardiovascular science and medicine and know the territory. This means we understand the research field and terminology, the journals and funding bodies you are targeting, your audience, and the challenge of peer-review. We are PhD scientists with years of laboratory research experience. We have secured grant funding, published in high-impact peer-reviewed journals [Circulation, Hypertension, JACC, European Heart Journal, Journal of Physiology (London), etc.], obtained ethical approval for research studies in the UK and USA, and acted as ad-hoc peer-reviewers for hundreds of manuscripts. We have worked on projects funded by the NIH in the USA and the Framework 7 Programme in the EU, from basic science experiments to multi-centre clinical trials. We have edited millions of words in the full range of scientific documents. We are native English speakers who have worked in leading research universities in the USA and UK and have collaborated with scientists around the World. We are also independent of any publishing house.

Work with us

Please get in touch if you are interested in joining our team. We are keen to hear from any of the following:

  • Scientists with publication and peer-review experience in journals that specialise in cardiovascular science and medicine.
  • People with an English Language background.
  • Digital Illustrators and people who are obsessed with data visualisation.
  • Data scientists with a good coding background who have a vision of how data science can be used to support the scientists we work with.

About our name and logo

The infinity symbol is comprised of two circles joined together and interconnected to create an infinite double-circuit. Likewise, the mammalian circulation is comprised of two circuits that interconnect at the heart: the pulmonary circuit that conducts blood to the lungs, and the systemic circulation that conducts blood around the body, in an 'infinite' double-circuit. Our logo, the infinite heart, incorporates the ideas of a heart and infinite double circulation. The red and blue colours allude to the traditional representation of oxygenated and de-oxygenated blood.
