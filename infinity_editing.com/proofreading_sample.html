After editing

Technical writing is fraught with difficulty. The most important ideas must stand out and attention to detail will be meticulously inspected. A spell-checker might help, but doesn't always sniff out spelling mistakes; and when auto-correction secretly changes nitric oxide to nitrous oxide, it's no laughing matter.

Good writing is clear, crisp and concise. It uses plain English. The composition allows a logical progression from sentence to sentence, paragraph to paragraph and between sections. There are no jarring errors or missed words to interrupt the reader. And the main arguments are unambiguous and easy to comprehend.

Our Proofreading service provides a fresh pair of eyes to find typing errors, homophones, and missing or misused words, as well as the usual checks for errors in grammar, spelling and punctuation.

Our Standard Editing will improve readability, consistency and clarity. Awkward and convoluted sentences will be converted into fluid, natural-sounding English that is easy to comprehend.

Premium Editing will reorganise disjointed paragraphs, reword sentences, remove repetition and reduce the word count. Writing that is cumbersome and hard to understand will be transformed into well-written, easy-to-read English with improved composition and structure.
