Editing and proofreading

Proofreading

  • This service will help you to present accurate work and save time by having an editor take care of the small details.
  • Use this service for minor corrections to a well-written document that needs a careful, final check.
  • We will correct typing errors, spelling, punctuation and basic grammar. We may edit words, but not sentences.
  • See an example of our services here (opens new tab).

Standard Editing

  • Improve the readability of your document with clear and concise content.
  • We will edit to improve sentence structure, clarity, coherence and conciseness.
  • Use this service to accelerate writing projects or if English is not your first language. Our most frequently used service.

Premium Editing

  • Fast-track projects and let us transform your work into a finished product.
  • We will edit and reorganise text to improve overall composition, style and readability.
  • Use this service when substantial help is needed to bring a project to completion.

You might want to use us to support particular aspects of your work or use us at pinch-points when workloads spike. It may be that some tasks impede progress and can be easily outsourced to us to increase productivity in a flexible, cost-effective way. If English is not your first language, we can help you overcome the barrier of technical scientific writing in English. Our overarching goal is your success and building a strong, productive relationship with you.

Our service is suitable for native and non-native English speakers who work in medicine, academia, pharmaceuticals, biomedical engineering, imaging and measurement, healthcare and laboratory technology development, commercial laboratories and communications. We can also help students with assignments, dissertations, and theses (this should be agreed with the course tutor/supervisor). If you are not sure if we can help you, get in touch and let us know what you have in mind.

We specialise in cardiovascular science and medicine and know the teritory. This means we understand the research field and terminology, the journals and funding bodies you are targeting, your audience, and the challenge of peer-review. We are PhD scientists with years of laboratory research experience. We have secured grant funding, published in high-impact peer-reviewed journals [Circulation, Hypertension, JACC, European Heart Journal, Journal of Physiology (London), etc.], obtained ethical approval for research studies in the UK and USA, conducted clinical trials, and acted as ad-hoc peer-reviewers for hundreds of manuscripts. We have worked on projects funded by the NIH in the USA and the Framework 7 Programme in the EU, from basic science experiments to multi-centre clinical trials. We have edited millions of words in the full range of scientific documents. We are native English speakers who have worked in leading research universities in the USA and UK and have collaborated with scientists around the World. We are also independent of any publishing house.

We specialise in cardiovascular science and medicine that broadly focuses on major cardiovascular diseases, diseases with vascular involvement, ageing, lifestyle, nutrition, basic science, clinical trials, healthcare devices, laboratory technology and physiology. We can help with all forms of written work, presentations, data visualisation and illustration. Please ask us if you have a specific query.

You will only be charged for the words that we edit. We will normally check and edit the words in the title, short-title, abstract/summary, keywords, main body of the text, figure legends and table headings. However, you can specify exactly what you would like us to edit if you wish. Text within the tables and figures are not edited. If you send us supplementary materials, we will include those in the edit. If there is text that you do not wish to be edited, please state this when submitting your documents (or redact the words in the documents). Title page content like author lists, affiliations and contact details will not be edited. We do not edit or format references unless requested as an additional service (please contact us). The word count will be included in a quote for your approval before work commences

We do not edit references as a standard part of the service, but this can be requested as an extra service if the references were managed using Endnote software. In this case, we will need the Endnote library used to format the references and we will edit the library and references as required. The edited library will be returned to you with the manuscript for future use. Please contact us about this and let us know how many references you have in your manuscript.

We will edit your work to try to make your message clear and accurate and reduce the potential for misunderstandings. If we are concerned about the factual accuracy of what you have written, we will query this with you (usually using the Comments function in Microsoft Word). However, the work must be your voice, your message and your responsibility. We cannot check the accuracy of your measurements, the data you report, calculations, statistical analysis, accurate and fair citation, or the validity of the underlying theory. If you have concerns about any of these aspects of your work, our consultation service may be of assistance with some of these things, but journal publishers, conference organisers and funding organisations will ask that you accept responsibility for the factual accuracy of your work before submission.

Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee that your research proposal will be funded or that your manuscript will be accepted for publication. Our purpose is to give you the best possible chance of success by demonstrating careful attention to detail and by helping you to communicate your work clearly and concisely using accurate, correct English.

Yes. You may have noticed that this site uses British English, but we are experienced in writing and editing in British and American English. The differences are subtle, but it is better to create a good impression and write in the form of English used by the journal to which you intend to submit.

We can accept documents prepared in Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx), Rich Text (.txt), LaTeX (.tex) or Scrivener (.scrivx) file formats. We can also accept documents written using HTML, but we will need to edit them and return them in a different file format (e.g., .txt). Microsoft Word has the advantage that we can use the Track Changes function to track the work we have done on your manuscript.

Images will be delivered in scalable vector graphics (.svg) format as well raster-based/bitmap formats of your choosing (.jpeg, .png, .gif, .tiff, etc.). Scalable vector graphics allow you to re-size an image without loss of quality or pixelation but require software that can use this format. Please ask us if you are not sure about this.

We accept payment by bank transfer. We can begin work as soon as you accept the price we have quoted, payment can be made at any time before we return the work to you.
